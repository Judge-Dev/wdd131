Abuja Nigeria

Abuja is the capital city of Nigeria, located right in the heart of the country. Known for its modern architecture, well-planned layout, and scenic landscapes, Abuja stands out as one of Africa’s most purpose-built capitals. It is home to iconic landmarks like Aso Rock, the Nigerian National Mosque, and the National Christian Centre. The city blends government, culture, and nature, offering a peaceful environment with a growing tech and education scene.
